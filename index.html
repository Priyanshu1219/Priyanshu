About City

Dubai is a city located in the United Arab Emirates. It is world-famous for its innovative architecture, gold market, oil wells, and energetic lifestyle. It started his journey as a small fishing and trading city, and it quickly grew into a major international hub all around the world. Dubai is well known for its futuristic skyline, which is made up of massive skyscrapers. The world's tallest structure, the Burj Khalifa, is a representation of the city's aspiration and creativity. Other noteworthy buildings are the sail-shaped Burj Al Arab and the twisted Cayan Tower.
